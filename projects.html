Research Projects

Regional studies of ocean fine structure, eddies, and mixing

Current and previous projects focus on subsurface mesoscale eddies in the Philippine Sea and Arctic Ocean, thermohaline staircases in the Caribbean Sea, and submesoscale structures revealed by seismic oceanography.

Philippine Sea Subsurface Mesoscale Eddies

This project investigates subsurface mesoscale eddies in the Philippine Sea using multichannel seismic reflection data, hydrographic observations, satellite altimetry, and ocean reanalysis products. The project aims to image fine thermohaline structures, estimate diapycnal mixing, and examine the influence of regional circulation systems.

Region
Philippine Sea
Data
MCS, CTD/XBT, satellite altimetry, reanalysis
Methods
Seismic imaging, hydrographic analysis, inversion, mixing estimation
Keywords
Subsurface mesoscale eddies; seismic oceanography; mixing; geostrophic flow

Arctic Mesoscale Eddies and Enhanced Mixing

This project focuses on mesoscale eddies in the western Arctic Ocean and investigates enhanced mixing near eddy edges using high-resolution seismic reflection data and oceanographic observations.

Region
Western Arctic Ocean, Chukchi Borderlands, Northwind Basin
Data
High-resolution seismic reflection data and hydrographic observations
Methods
Seismic section interpretation, eddy structure analysis, mixing estimation
Keywords
Mesoscale eddies; enhanced mixing; internal waves; Arctic Ocean

Thermohaline Staircases in the Eastern Caribbean Sea

This project studies thermohaline staircases and their disruption by subsurface mesoscale eddies in the eastern Caribbean Sea. The work combines seismic oceanography and hydrographic observations to reveal fine-scale ocean structures.

Region
Eastern and Southeast Caribbean Sea
Data
Seismic reflection sections and hydrographic observations
Methods
Fine-structure imaging, thermohaline staircase interpretation, eddy-staircase interaction analysis
Keywords
Thermohaline staircases; subsurface eddies; fine structure; stratification

Submesoscale Structures and Internal Waves

These studies use marine seismic survey data to investigate submesoscale eddies, fronts, and mode-2 internal solitary waves near Central America, the Antarctic Peninsula, and adjacent regions.

Regions
Gulf of Papagayo, Pacific coast of Central America, South Shetland Islands
Data
Marine seismic survey data and supporting oceanographic datasets
Methods
Seismic oceanographic interpretation, wave structure analysis, frontal and vortex characterization
Keywords
Submesoscale eddies; fronts; internal solitary waves; seismic oceanography
